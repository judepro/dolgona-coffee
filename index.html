dolgona coffee

This yummy south korean coffee that's all over the internet requires four ingrediants to make - all of which you probably have in the cupboard - you feel like you've won the jackpot, right?

ingrediants

  • instant coffee
  • sugar
  • hot water
  • milk

      • dolgona making process

      step1:combine instant coffee sugar and hot water
      Add 2 tablespoons of instant coffee, 2 tablespoons of sugar and 2 tablespoons of hot water to a medium mixing bow

      step 2: whisk whisk whisk until the coffee is light and fluffy
      Using an electric hand-held mixer, whip the coffee mixture until it is light brown, fluffy, and holds stiff peaks when the whisk is removed.

      Step 3: Serve with milk
      Use a spoon to garnish a glass of hot or cold milk with coffee cream. Serve with a straw, an stir , stir, stir to enjoy.
